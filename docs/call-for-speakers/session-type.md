# Session Type

- Session Presentation: 30 minutes, 1-2 speaker(s) presenting on a topic
- Panel Discussion: 30 minutes of discussion amongst 3 to 5 speakers (with a moderator appointed by KCD Kuala Lumpur)
- Lightning Talk: A brief 5-minute presentation, maximum of 1 speaker
- Workshop: 60-minute, in-depth, hands-on presentation with 1-3 speakers
